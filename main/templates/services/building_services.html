Building services

At AMAC Engineering, we offer a range of building services to improve and maintain both new and existing buildings. We help with office partitions, ceiling and tile installations, painting, and interior decoration.

We also handle aluminum works, waterproofing to prevent leaks, and all types of plumbing jobs. Whether it’s a full renovation or small repairs, we aim to deliver high-quality results that meet our clients’ needs.
