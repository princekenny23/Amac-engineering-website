Mechanical Services

AMAC Engineering provides a wide range of mechanical services designed to support industries and infrastructure development. Our team works on both small and large projects with precision and care.

We design and build steel structures, install tanks and piping systems for water and petroleum, and handle HVAC systems like air conditioning and ventilation. We also install elevators, escalators, and industrial machines, as well as fabricate and install duct systems. Our goal is to ensure safe, efficient, and durable mechanical solutions for every client.
